# NYC Tech-Startup Job Targets

Researched June 2026. These are NYC (or NYC-office) startup roles that fit a
tech-led profile with a genuine people-person, improv, and performance edge:
backend/Linux/infra credibility plus the communication, content, and on-stage
skills that make developer relations work.

Each resume variant in this repo mirrors the target posting's own language
selectively (one or two echoes, never parroted). Build them with
`nix build '.#resume'`; outputs land as `<dest>-resume.pdf`.

> Caveat: a few posting pages blocked direct fetching, so phrasing below was
> reconstructed from search excerpts and job-board mirrors (LinkedIn,
> ZipRecruiter, Ashby, Lever). Re-check each live posting before applying in
> case a role has closed or changed.

## 1. Writer — Developer Advocate, Events & Social (NYC)

- Wants an "accomplished storyteller with a strong technical background"
- Represent Writer at engineering/research events, on social, and in AI
  engineering communities (Discord, Reddit); run hackathons, workshops,
  podcasts, and streams; Python + AI/ML communities
- "putting the audience first by ensuring content is accessible and engaging"
- 3+ years as a developer advocate/engineer with public speaking and community
  engagement; travel 30–50%
- Why it fits: improv and live-performance background maps directly onto the
  events, speaking, and social mandate.
- Resume: `resumes/writer.typ` → `writer-resume.pdf`
- Source: LinkedIn 4217712122 / jobs.ashbyhq.com/writer
