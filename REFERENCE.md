## Voice / Tone Reference (for new variant copy)

When writing job descriptions or summaries for a new variant, match the register of the audience:

- **Tech:** direct, precise, named tools, real numbers. No "leveraged" or "spearheaded."
- **Hospitality / Salt & Straw:** energetic, grounded, casual. "Got comfortable making calls on my own." First-person implied.
- **Childcare / Nanny:** warm, patient, specific to kids. First-person is acceptable here.
- **Acting / Artistic:** confident, specific about roles and training. Industry-standard formatting.
- **Events / Work (general):** competent, slightly casual. Performer empathy + technician precision.

Avoid: hollow intensifiers (leveraged, utilized, spearheaded, orchestrated), stacked buzzwords, symmetrical bullet structure, vague impact claims, thesaurus cycling for "managed," em dashes (use hyphens or commas).

When tailoring to a posting: echo the posting's *concepts*, not its exact phrasing. One or two keyword echoes per description, scattered across entries.
